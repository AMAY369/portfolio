### **Pro Tips for Higher Response Rates:**

1. **Personalize Each Email:**
   - Mention specific project details
   - Reference a memorable moment or challenge
   - Show genuine appreciation for working together

2. **Make It Easy:**
   - Provide specific questions (not just "write a testimonial")
   - Offer to draft it for them to edit
   - Give permission options upfront (anonymize if needed)

3. **Follow Up Strategy:**
   - Send email first
   - After 48 hours: LinkedIn message reminder
   - After 5 days: One final gentle follow-up
   - After 7 days: Move to next priority contact

4. **Timing Matters:**
   - Send Tuesday-Thursday mornings (best response rates)
   - Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)
